Lesson 2 (Live Analysis) 30/07/2023

Focus Areas:

  • When going for shots try to make sure your crosshair is as close to the enemies head as possible instead of shooting first and adjusting later.
  • Try to always position yourself within the optimal range of the weapon you are playing with close range with SMG/Pistol/Shotgun and far with scout and awp.
  • Always be weary of the number of bullets you have left in your mag as running out of bullets mid-fight can be a very frustrating way to die.
  • Whenever the bomb is planted you want to execute the retake as quickly as possible because the longer the bomb is planted the more pressure it puts on you to do the retake rushed.
  • Double-clearing angles wastes time and energy during this game you double-cleared a few angles causing your crosshair to stay on a safe angle for longer than necessary and required you to do more input.

FFA Community-Deathmatch Notes

  • Tapping is quite good but often missing the first shot, due to not lining it up properly and adjusting off of pure reactions after that.
  • When headshot only mode for pistols got turned off you started focusing a lot more on bodyshots which wasnâ€™t the right direction, when on pistol rounds headshots reign supreme so you want to make sure you focus on getting them.
